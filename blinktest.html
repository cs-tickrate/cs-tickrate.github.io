TICKRATE TEST

About

This website was made for you to test whether you can spot any difference between delay caused by 64 and 128 tickrate in Counter-Strike.

How it works

  • Below is a clickable black space

  • You can select multiple options for your tests

    • Simulated tickrate - tickrate, from which the length of the flash will be calculated (highest delay possible)

    • Loop:

      • off = after clicking, the site black area will flash pink once

      • on = after clicking, the black area will keep switching between black and pink until you click again

    • Guessing mode - makes the tickrate random (64 or 128) and you will be able to guess which one it is. The correct answer will be shown to you after your guess.
